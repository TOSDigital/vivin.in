VIVIN sounds as "we-win”, we aim to win with our customers alongside expertise and the talent at our disposal

VIVIN Group is a construction & a real estate company with innovation at its core. We aim at building a healthier lifestyle for the city-dwellers and greater sustainability for the ecosystem. The years that followed saw the company making several firsts in the industry – all directed towards achieving this single-minded goal. We have diversified our portfolio by working with several industries and we have undertaken commercial projects and have created a beautiful workspace and sustainable projects. VIVIN Group has diverse interests in real estate, industrial contracting, and infrastructure development.

Vision

At VIVIN we want to be a part of the infrastructure innovation and growth . VIVIN shall be a beacon of honesty and hard work and settle for nothing less than top class customer satisfaction in quality, reliability, timely completion and a safe working environment.

Our existence is to make the entire process of investment for individuals and businesses as smooth and transparent as possible with an ethical business practice & collaborative, creative environment.

Every year we aim at achieving double the growth of previous year. We have always been successful in doing that and intend to keep it that way.

Corporate Social Responsibility

Corporate Social Responsibility is becoming increasingly important to today’s candidates. Companies that offer a workplace and corporate culture of caring about essential social issues can be successful at both business and giving back to the wider community. With that in mind, we are one of the socially responsible companies currently doing to make an impact to needy.
